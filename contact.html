Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about our credit repair services

Is the consultation really free?

Yes! Our initial consultation is completely free with no obligation. We'll analyze your credit situation and provide recommendations without any cost to you.

How long does credit repair take?

Most clients see significant improvements within 3-6 months, though results vary based on individual circumstances. We provide regular updates throughout the process.

What information do I need for the consultation?

For the initial consultation, we just need basic contact information and a general understanding of your credit goals. We'll help you get your credit reports during the process.

Do you guarantee results?

While we can't guarantee specific results, we have a 95% success rate with our clients. We're committed to helping you achieve your credit goals and will work tirelessly on your behalf.

Is credit repair legal?

Absolutely! Credit repair is completely legal. The Fair Credit Reporting Act gives you the right to dispute inaccurate information on your credit reports. We help you exercise these rights effectively.

What if I'm not satisfied with the service?

We offer a satisfaction guarantee. If you're not completely satisfied with our services, we'll work to make it right or provide a refund according to our terms.
